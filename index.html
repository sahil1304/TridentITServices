Trident IT Services

At Trident IT Services, we offer a complete range of IT solutions including buying and selling laptops/desktops, laptop rentals, onsite AMC services, antivirus software, printer toner refilling, CCTV and biometric solutions, as well as software and website development.

Our Features

At Trident IT Services, we provide comprehensive IT solutions designed to meet the diverse needs of individuals and businesses. From hardware sales to advanced security systems and software development, our services are crafted to deliver reliability, efficiency, and value.
Laptop/Desktop Buying and Selling:

We offer a wide range of new and pre-owned laptops and desktops from top brands at competitive prices. Our experts help you choose the right device based on your needs and budget. All devices are thoroughly tested to ensure quality and performance.

Laptop Rental Services:

Need a laptop for a short-term project, business event, or startup operations? We provide flexible rental plans for laptops and desktops at affordable rates. Our rental services ensure you always have access to the latest technology without heavy upfront costs.

Onsite AMC (Annual Maintenance Contract) Services:

Keep your IT systems running at their best with our comprehensive AMC services. We offer regular maintenance, quick troubleshooting, and onsite technical support to minimize downtime and improve system performance. Our plans are customized to fit different business requirements.

Antivirus and Software Solutions:

Protect your systems with trusted antivirus solutions and licensed software packages. We supply and install the latest software to safeguard your data, optimize your systems, and ensure smooth daily operations. Our team also provides support for software upgrades and renewals.

Printer Toner Refilling:

Save costs without compromising print quality with our professional toner refilling services. We handle cartridges for all major printer brands and ensure proper refilling to extend cartridge life. Our services help businesses reduce printing expenses significantly.

CCTV and Biometric Solutions:

Enhance the security of your premises with our advanced CCTV surveillance systems and biometric access controls. We handle everything from consultation and installation to maintenance, ensuring your property and data remain protected at all times.

Software and Website Development:

At Trident IT Services, we specialize in building powerful software applications and dynamic websites that help businesses streamline operations and strengthen their online presence. Our team of skilled developers works closely with clients to understand their specific needs and deliver tailor-made solutions, whether it's a custom business management tool, a mobile app, an e-commerce platform, or a corporate website.
We focus on creating user-friendly, secure, scalable, and SEO-optimized websites that ensure excellent performance across all devices. Our software solutions are designed to improve efficiency, enhance customer engagement, and drive business growth. From initial consultation to deployment and ongoing support, we are with you at every step to ensure your digital success.
